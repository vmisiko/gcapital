Peer To Peer Grants

G-Capital Grants allows people to lend small amounts of money directly to entrepreneurs in developing countries. It is a peer to peer micro-lending service that links borrowers and lenders worldwide without the inter mediation of a local micro-finance institution.

G-Capital Grants facilitates micro-lending transactions between individual funding Partners worldwide and low-income entrepreneurs in developing countries. Partners fund the loans, which borrowers use to develop their young businesses. Borrowers may write their own business proposal and upload it on our website. However an SMS based questionare is also available and a Processing fee of Ksh 100 is charged per Every Application to cater for administering the Grant and SMS cost.

INVEST IN YOUR BUSINESS GROWTH NOW!

Send an SMS with the word GRANT to 20257 to Apply for A Free Grant. After paying the Application Fee, Fill out your Grant Application via SMS/Web

The interest rate for G-Capital Grants is 0% and a processing fee of 3.5% is chargeable on Disbursement, Funding above Ksh 50,000 is wired into Borrowers Account or a Cheque is issued for Collection at the Nairobi Office. Applications approved and Less than Ksh 50,000 are Disbursed via M-PESA. Potential applicants must have a business or source of income that will allow them to make repayments each week; Be able to secure endorsements from their family, neighbors or work associates, and the leader of an organization in their community.

If you have been invited to join by an existing member in good standing, you can borrow up to 5000 KES for your first loan. If you do not have an invite from an existing member, you can borrow up to Ksh 2,000 for your first loan. After that, your credit limit will increase depending on your record of on-time repayments.
