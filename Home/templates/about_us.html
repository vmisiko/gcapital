About G-Capital Grants

G-Capital Grants strengthens the global startup community by connecting entrepreneurs creating innovative and scalable businesses with the knowledge, network and funding they require to succeed.

THE SHORT-LISTING PROCESS

Sign Up Now

The Africa Capital Grants' Directors appoint a Grants Committee to undertake a shortlisting and final selection process. For every Grants round, hundreds of applications are usually received and the committee can only select a small percentage to fund. We will notify the successful applicants at individual level with the outcome of their assesment process. However, the unsuccessful applicants will receive a general regret message.
